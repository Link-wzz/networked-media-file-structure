Inspiration

When I came up with this website, I wanted to show some of my photography. When I was thinking about mediums that could carry photos, the first thing that came to my mind was Lotte Koala's March Bite Sized Cookies, a kind of snack I used to eat as a kid, which was basically a chocolate chip cookie in the shape of a cute koala. The reason why I thought of this snack is that every time you buy a box of these biscuits, you can tear off a small piece of paper at the bottom of the biscuit box, about 1.5*2inch in size, and there is a cute photo of a koala on the small pieces of paper, you can collect these photos, and you can also put them together to form a koala family photo.

So I thought I could make a mechanism to buy cookies and put my photograph in it as gift. But the cookie box was a bit hard to distribute, so then I thought of a vending machine:


Process

Vending machine is a great medium, it can distribute different kinds of drinks, and it is relatively easy to make cartoon shapes. Therefore the final website would be about a Vending machine that offers free drinks, and with each drink, the user can receive a postcard of my photography. After deciding the theme, I used figma to draw the vending Machine and the drinks in it:

Then I bring all the stuff to the PS and use it to make shadow effects. I also made the note ui in the PS


Then, when all the visual icons are done, I start to put the things together in HTML and CSS. The coding process is not much to say, I keep trying the code and then looking for ways to debug.
